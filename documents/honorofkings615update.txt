6/17 S15 The Grand Venture Version Update Announcement

Hi there,

A non-disruptive global server update is planned for 6/17 at 00:00–01:00 (UTC±0).

 

Update Time:

6/17 from 00:00–01:00 (UTC±0)

The game will begin the season data maintenance 1.5 hours before the update. During this period, players cannot play ranked/Peak Tournament matches. Matches that begin before this will not be affected.

 

After the update has been launched, players can manually choose whether they want to install it. Players who did not update the game can still play, but will be unable to join Ranked/Peak Tournament matches or form premades with players who did install the update.




Update Scope: All servers


Update Method: Live server update

 

Update Content

1. The new season 15, The Grand Venture, will officially launch on 6/17.

2. Four new heroes—Thunderous Deep: Devara, Gentle Blade: Florentino, Nightmare Incarnate: Lorion, and Diviner: Annette have arrived!

3. A new Mythic skin, Avant-Garde Director Yaria, is here. Point Draw discounts are coming!

4. Hero Balance Adjustments:

a) Mechanics Upgraded: Sima Yi and Li Xin

b) Stats Buffed: Lian Po, Ming, Zhuangzi, and Augran

Bounty Match
A special season of Bounty Gauntlet is here!

1. Season Duration: 7/02–7/26 (available for four weekends during the event)

2. The Bounty Gauntlet is divided into three levels: Standard, Advanced, and Elite. Winning it guarantees a Championship-limited treasure and a random treasure. The value of the treasure is strongly correlated with the level of the Bounty Gauntlet.

i) Standard Championship: Tickets are free, and the value of the treasures is relatively low.

ii) Advanced Championship: Purchase tickets using event currency from The Grand Venture event. The rewards are of moderate value.

iii) Elite Championship: Purchase tickets using Tokens. The reward for winning is guaranteed to be far greater than the ticket price, and even if you lose, you will receive a guaranteed return.

3. Tickets cannot be changed after each round. You can play a total of 2 rounds of matches each week and win the Championship a maximum of 2 times.

Adjusted or new talent effects

The following is a partial preview:

1) Extra Skills

Vacuity Lock: Hurls a chain hook in the target direction, pulling enemies towards the user and dealing true damage to them.

Winning Strategy: Fires an orb at the target, dealing grievous damage. If a hero is defeated, this skill's cooldown is reduced.

Flash: Flashes a certain distance in the target direction. 20s cooldown.




2) Treasure

Rare Vault: Grants 1 random mid-game talent.

Epic Vault: Grants 2 random early-game talents.

Legendary Vault: Grants 1 random late-game talent.

Mythic Vault: Grants 2 random mid-game talents.




3) Communication

Call the Play: Enhances all teammates when sending a quick chat. 15s cooldown. "Attack" corresponds to Frenzy, "Gather" corresponds to Sprint, and "Retreat" corresponds to Purify.

Mic On: When using the microphone, emits sound waves that deal damage to enemies and heal allies. The higher the microphone volume, the greater the effect.




4) Gold

Healing Fees: When healing or providing a shield for teammates, grants 1 Gold for every 20 Health restored or 50 shield value granted.

Buyback: Usable after being defeated. Consumes 3,000 Gold to instantly resurrect. 60s cooldown.

Pay to Win: When dealing damage, consumes 1% of current Gold to fire an extra projectile at the target. The more Gold consumed, the higher the damage. 1s cooldown.




5) Other New Talents

Shadow Buddy: Summons a Shadow Vanguard that grows alongside you.

Return Fire: Reflects 25% of damage taken.

Speed Incantation: When dealing skill damage, steals target's Movement Speed.

Every Step Counts: Each time a certain distance is traveled, deals damage to nearby enemies.
